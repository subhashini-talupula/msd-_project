Creamy Tomato Pasta

Tags: vegetarian, 30m

Ingredients

  • pasta
  • tomato
  • cream
  • basil
  • parmesan

Steps

  1. Boil pasta until al dente.
  2. Make a tomato sauce with garlic and cream.
  3. Combine pasta and sauce, garnish with basil and parmesan.
